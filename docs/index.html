About Earth & Our Project

Earth is our shared home, and clean air is essential for life. Rising pollution levels threaten ecosystems, human health, and climate stability. Our project uses AI to monitor air quality, detect harmful gases, and raise awareness about environmental protection.

How AI is Used

Artificial Intelligence processes sensor data from the MQ-135 gas sensor and ESP32 microcontroller. It learns patterns of air pollution, predicts future trends, and provides real-time alerts through the LED ring and dashboards.

Materials Used

ESP32 Board

A powerful Wi-Fi and Bluetooth enabled microcontroller used as the brain of the project.

DFPlayer Mini

A small audio module that can play MP3 files from a microSD card, useful for alerts.

Arduino UNO

A versatile microcontroller board used for prototyping and controlling sensors and modules.

WS2812 RGB LED Ring

Programmable LEDs that visually display air quality levels with color-coded signals.
